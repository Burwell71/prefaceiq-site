How it works

Three chapters, before every call.

PrefaceIQ doesn't just remind you a meeting is happening. It gives you the page you'd want read before it starts.

Chapter One

Add the meeting

Drop in a name, a company, a time. Sync your calendar and PrefaceIQ picks up the rest on its own.

Chapter Two

Read the brief

A short, sourced brief lands before you do — their role, what's changed recently at their company, and a real opening line, not a script.

Chapter Three

Walk in ready

Take notes during the call, and PrefaceIQ carries them into the next one — so no relationship starts from zero twice.

What's inside

Built for the calls that actually matter.

AI-generated briefs

Every meeting gets a short brief written specifically for it — who you're meeting, their world right now, and a natural way in. No generic talking points.

Calendar sync

Connect your calendar once. New meetings show up automatically, briefed and ready before you've had coffee.

Meeting history

Past meetings stay on record, dimmed but searchable — so the second conversation with someone always starts further ahead than the first.

Reminders that matter

A nudge before the call, not a wall of notifications. PrefaceIQ tells you once, at the right moment, with the brief already attached.
